Privacy Policy – Locus Brose Adapter

Last updated: June 2026

1. Overview

Locus Brose Adapter ("the app") is an Android application that acts as a Bluetooth sensor adapter between a Brose e-bike motor and Locus Maps. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

2. Bluetooth permissions

The app uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to communicate directly and exclusively with your Brose e-bike motor. This connection is local, device-to-device only. No data is sent to any server, third party, or cloud service.

  • BLUETOOTH_SCAN – to discover your motor via BLE
  • BLUETOOTH_CONNECT – to read live telemetry from your motor

The flag neverForLocation is set on the scan permission: the app does not use Bluetooth for location purposes.

3. Data we do NOT collect

  • No personal information (name, email, account)
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or crash reporting
  • No advertising identifiers

4. Third-party services

The app does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics libraries, or advertising networks.

5. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be sent to: hoschilo@gmail.com
